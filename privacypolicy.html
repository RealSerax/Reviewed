Privacy policy

Last updated on 20th January



END DATA?

We are not storing any personal data except

user IDs from those who rated to check if they

can rate the product again or not.

Aswell we are not storing message content.


DATA SHARING?

We do not share any data.

The data we collect is only for feature purpose,

and we do not access it.

Though, features like overall rating calculation

requires us to share the rated stars. You dont see from

who the stars were.


WHICH DATA DO WE COLLECT?

User IDs to not allow multiple ratings by the same user on the same product

Guild IDs to let every Server have their own settings and own products

Custom variables like given stars, reviews, product names, customizations you set.

(TO CLARIFY: THIS DOES NOT INCLUDE THE REVIEWS COMMENTS, ONLY THE REVIEWS RATING)


DATA DELETION?

Currently you can request your data to be deleted with

opening a ticket on our support discord Server. Though, we will create a /deletedata command

so you can delete it easier by yourself soon.


POLICY UPDATES?

We can update our policy with changes that wont harm your data,

and if we do so, we will let you know on our Support discord Server.
